About HellasNOC

Our Story

Founded in 2014, HellasNOC has been at the forefront of providing professional IT services to businesses across Greece. What started as a small team of passionate network engineers has grown into a comprehensive IT solutions provider trusted by hundreds of companies.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make enterprise-grade IT infrastructure accessible to businesses of all sizes. Over the years, we've helped companies transform their technology landscape, secure their networks, and scale their operations with confidence.

Today, HellasNOC stands as a leading provider of network management, cybersecurity, and cloud solutions, serving clients from small startups to large enterprises across various industries.
