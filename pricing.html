Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

No setup fees for Professional and Enterprise plans. Starter plan includes a one-time setup fee of €99.

Support includes remote assistance, troubleshooting, system monitoring, and regular maintenance tasks.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes, we provide custom solutions for Enterprise clients. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your subscription with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts required.
